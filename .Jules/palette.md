## 2024-12-19 - Accessibility issues with material-symbols-outlined icon-only buttons
**Learning:** Icon-only buttons using `material-symbols-outlined` as text ligatures in this application consistently miss `aria-label` attributes on the parent `<button>` and `aria-hidden="true"` on the icon itself. This leads to poor screen reader accessibility as the ligature text (e.g., 'menu') may be spoken instead of the button's action, or nothing helpful at all. Also, there's a lack of explicit keyboard focus visibility on these buttons which breaks accessibility expectations.
**Action:** When working on components that use `material-symbols-outlined` inside `button` elements, particularly for icon-only instances, always explicitly include `aria-label` on the button, `aria-hidden="true"` on the span, and proper focus styles (e.g., `focus-visible:ring-2 focus-visible:ring-primary focus:outline-none`).
